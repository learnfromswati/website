Cancellation & Refund Policy

English by Swati offers limited refunds as classes are personalized and batch-based.

  • Fees once paid are non-refundable after class commencement.
  • In case of cancellation before the first class, a 90% refund will be issued within 7 working days.
  • Demo class fees (if any) are non-refundable.
  • In rare cases where classes are cancelled by the instructor, the full unused portion of the fee will be refunded.

For refund requests, please write to support@englishbyswati.com within 7 days of payment.
